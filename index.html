Sleek — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This policy explains what data the Sleek game (“we”, “the app”) collects, why, and how you can control it.

What we collect

  • Advertising identifier — via Google AdMob, to show ads and measure ad performance. Personalised ads are used only where you have consented (see Your choices below).
  • Approximate (IP-derived) location — used by AdMob and analytics for ad delivery and coarse regional metrics. We do not collect GPS location.
  • An anonymous account identifier — Firebase Anonymous Authentication assigns a random ID to your install so your leaderboard entries and settings can be associated with you. It is not linked to your name, email, or any real-world identity.
  • Gameplay analytics — via Firebase Analytics: events such as level starts/completions, ad views, and purchases, to understand and improve the game.
  • Crash & performance diagnostics — via Firebase Crashlytics and Performance Monitoring, to find and fix bugs.
  • Purchase history — records of in-app coin-bundle purchases, to deliver what you bought and to prevent duplicate credits.
  • Leaderboard display name — an auto-generated name (e.g. “Swift Falcon”) plus a short slice of your anonymous ID. There is no free-text name entry, so no personal information is shown on leaderboards.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location, and we do not send push notifications or sell your data.

Third parties

The app uses Google services (AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring, Cloud Firestore). Their handling of data is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy. Ads are served by Google and its partner networks.

Your choices

  • EU/UK/Swiss users: on first launch you are shown a Google-certified consent form (UMP) to accept or decline personalised ads. You can change this any time via Settings → Privacy options.
  • iOS users: iOS asks for App Tracking Transparency permission; you can change it in the system Settings for the app.
  • Delete your data: Settings → Delete my data erases your local save and removes your leaderboard entries and account records. You may also request deletion by emailing the address below.

Children

The app is intended for players 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data retention

Leaderboard entries and your anonymous account persist until you delete your data (Settings → Delete my data) or request deletion.
